I kept seeing the same problem in AI workflows:

A task starts in one tool, but the moment it moves to another tool, team member, or session, the important context gets diluted or lost.

So I put together **CRUMB** 🍞 — a simple, text-first AI handoff format.

The idea is straightforward:
Instead of transferring a full raw transcript, transfer a small structured block containing the goal, the relevant context, and the constraints.

That makes handoffs easier to:
- read
- validate
- paste into another AI
- preserve over time

It is early, but the repo is now live:
https://github.com/XioAISolutions/crumb-format

Would love feedback from people working with multi-tool AI workflows, coding agents, and long-running AI tasks.
